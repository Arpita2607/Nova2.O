About Us

Welcome to VedaCare – Where Ancient Wisdom Meets Modern Wellness

Vedacare is a blessing for the Ayurvedic dietitians which is an easy to use cloud- based software with simple and clean UI.

It is a site which helps patients with a personal diet based on their mental,physical and emotional health. Our aim is to bring Ayurveda into everyday life in a simple , modern and efficient way

The platform helps dietitians create personal meal plans, track nutrients, and manage patient records all in one place .Vedacare is here to support both dietitians and patients on their journey to healthy living
